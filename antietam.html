Originally from Canton, China, Pierce’s origins remain unclear. It is believed that he was sold into slavery as a young age and brought to the United States as a child. Pierce enlisted in the army at the age of 20 and joined the Fourteenth Connecticut Infantry regiment. During the battle of Antietam, in addition to his regiment’s heavy casualties, Pierce tripped over a fence during the fierce fighting and injured his back. Pierce was forced to withdraw and recover at a military hospital for the following year.
